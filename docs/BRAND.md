## Brand Essence

**Brand Name**: AskBob.Ai
**Tagline**: Your in-game OSRS expert.
**Brand Promise**: Instant, accurate OSRS knowledge without leaving the game.
**Core Values**: Accuracy, Speed, Accessibility, Community

---

## Brand Personality

The brand personality is inspired by **Bob the Cat** — the iconic, mysterious, and surprisingly knowledgeable cat found wandering around RuneScape. Bob the Cat has seen it all, traveled the world, and knows more than he lets on. He is casual, witty, and slightly aloof — but always has the answer when you need it. The AI assistant channels Bob's energy: approachable, effortlessly knowledgeable, and a beloved part of the OSRS world.

- **Character Reference**: Bob the Cat (wanders Gielinor, former human Robert the Strong, reincarnated as a cat)
- **Personality Traits**: Casual wisdom, dry wit, quietly omniscient, helpful when asked
- **Vibe**: Like chatting with a chill, all-knowing cat who has been everywhere and done everything

## Voice & Tone

### Brand Voice
- **Knowledgeable**: Speaks with authority about OSRS — like Bob the Cat who has seen it all across Gielinor
- **Helpful**: Always focused on solving the player's problem, not showing off
- **Concise**: Gives the answer first, details second — respects the player's time

### Tone by Context
| Context | Tone | Example |
|---------|------|---------|
| General answers | Friendly expert | "The best spot for mining iron is at the Mining Guild — you get an invisible +7 boost and the rocks respawn faster." |
| Quest help | Step-by-step guide | "For Monkey Madness, head to the Grand Tree first. Talk to King Narnode on the top floor." |
| Error/Unknown | Honest, helpful | "I'm not sure about that one. Try checking the wiki directly for the latest info." |

### Words to Use
- Direct game terminology (DPS, tick, spec, GE, etc.)
- OSRS community language naturally

### Words to Avoid
- "Actually..." → Just give the answer
- Overly formal language → Keep it like talking to a fellow player

## Visual Identity

### Plugin Panel Design Philosophy
The plugin panel should **fit seamlessly into RuneLite's dark theme** while having distinctive AI/futuristic elements that make it stand out:
- Subtle glow effects on active elements
- Smooth animations on message transitions
- AI-themed accent lighting
- Clean, readable text over the dark background

### Colors
| Name | Hex | Usage |
|------|-----|-------|
| RuneLite Dark BG | `#252525` | Primary background (match RuneLite) |
| RuneLite Panel BG | `#1b1b1b` | Panel background |
| AI Accent Glow | `#00e5ff` | Cyan glow for AI-themed elements, borders, active states |
| AI Accent Secondary | `#7c4dff` | Purple accent for highlights, hover states |
| Text Primary | `#e0e0e0` | Main text color (match RuneLite) |
| Text Secondary | `#9e9e9e` | Muted text, timestamps |
| Success | `#4caf50` | Positive states |
| Error | `#f44336` | Error states |

### Typography
| Level | Font | Size | Weight |
|-------|------|------|--------|
| Panel Title | RuneScape UF (or system sans-serif) | 14px | Bold |
| Chat Messages | System sans-serif (match RuneLite) | 12px | Regular |
| Timestamps | System sans-serif | 10px | Light |

### Spacing Scale
`xs`: 4px | `sm`: 8px | `md`: 12px | `lg`: 16px | `xl`: 24px
